### Understanding AI Results

**Every AI analysis provides:**

#### **Entry Point**
The recommended price to enter the trade.

**Example**: EUR/USD - Entry at 1.0845
- If buying: Enter when price reaches 1.0845
- If selling: Enter when price reaches 1.0845

#### **Stop Loss (SL)**
The price at which to exit if trade goes against you.

**Example**: Stop Loss at 1.0825
- Limits your loss to 20 pips
- Protects your capital
- Automatically exits position

#### **Take Profit (TP)**
The price at which to exit with profit.

**Example**: Take Profit at 1.0885
- Locks in profit of 40 pips
- Automated profit-taking
- May have multiple TP levels (TP1, TP2, TP3)

#### **Confidence Score**
AI's confidence in the analysis (0-100%).

**Interpreting Confidence:**
- **90-100%**: Very high confidence - strong signal
- **70-89%**: High confidence - good signal
- **50-69%**: Moderate confidence - proceed with caution
- **Below 50%**: Low confidence - avoid or wait

#### **Risk:Reward Ratio (R:R)**
Potential profit vs potential loss ratio.

**Example**: R:R of 2:1
- Risk: $100 (SL distance)
- Reward: $200 (TP distance)
- For every $1 risked, potential to gain $2

**Minimum Recommended R:R**: 1.5:1 or higher

#### **AI Explanation**
Detailed reasoning behind the analysis:
- Market structure analysis
- Key support/resistance levels
- Trend direction and strength
- Entry timing rationale
- Risk factors to consider

### Understanding P&L Calculations

**How Profit/Loss is Calculated:**

```
P&L = (Current Price - Entry Price) Ã— Position Size Ã— Pip Value

For BUY positions:
Profit when Current Price > Entry Price

For SELL positions:
Profit when Current Price < Entry Price
```

**Example:**
```
Entry: EUR/USD BUY at 1.0850
Current Price: 1.0870
Position Size: 1 standard lot (100,000 units)
Pip Value: $10

P&L = (1.0870 - 1.0850) Ã— 100,000 Ã— 0.0001 = 20 pips Ã— $10 = $200 profit
```

**Percentage P&L:**
```
P&L % = (P&L / Initial Investment) Ã— 100
```

**Unrealized vs Realized P&L:**
- **Unrealized**: Current profit/loss while trade is open
- **Realized**: Final profit/loss after closing trade
